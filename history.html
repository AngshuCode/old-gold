Early Beginnings (1960s - 1980s)

  • 1969 - **ARPANET**, the first version of the Internet, was created by the U.S. Department of Defense.
  • 1971 - The first **email** was sent by Ray Tomlinson.
  • 1983 - **TCP/IP** became the standard for Internet communication.

The Web Revolution (1990s)

  • 1991 - **Tim Berners-Lee** created the first website and the World Wide Web.
  • 1993 - **Mosaic**, the first graphical web browser, was released.
  • 1998 - **Google** was founded.

Modern Internet (2000s - Today)

  • 2004 - **Facebook** launched.
  • 2005 - **YouTube** was created.
  • 2007 - The **iPhone** revolutionized mobile Internet.
  • Today - Billions of people use the Internet daily!
